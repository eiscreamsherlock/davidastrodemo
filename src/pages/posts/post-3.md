- WWwise Fundamentals - [Free Tutorial](https://www.audiokinetic.com/en/learning/learn-wwise/wwise-fundamentals/)
- WWise Interactive Music - [Also Free Tutorial](https://www.audiokinetic.com/en/learning/learn-wwise/interactive-music/)
- The Odin Project [Foundations](https://www.theodinproject.com/paths/foundations/courses/foundations)

- Godot Game Engine Through [Zenva Academy](https://academy.zenva.com/course/humble-bundle-learn-godot-2026-tier-3/)

    I picked up this learning pack through Humble Bundle.  The first couple of projects were enough to get my feet wet with the engine, so I expect to have a pretty good handle on making things by the time the course is wrapped up.
- Ear Training and Composer Foundations at [Scoreclub](https://scoreclub.net/)

    There is a LOT to learn through this platform and it will likely take me much longer to complete than just the end of June.  But I'm hoping to at least complete the ear training and foundations sections to have a little more confidence in writing out musical ideas on paper (rather than needing to record them into the DAW in order to "save them for later").
